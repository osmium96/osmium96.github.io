about me

My name is Osman (pronounced uhs-MAHN). I was born in Fort Worth, Texas, and am now based in Palo Alto, California.

I am currently an Internal Medicine resident physician at Stanford. I also research health policy for novel medical technologies, in particular focusing on the intersection of innovation, regulation, and payment. Prior to residency, I completed my undergraduate studies in Chemical Physics and Biology at Columbia and medical school at Yale.
